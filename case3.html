Bee Crazy

This project we had to design a service that helped biodiversity and it had to include AI in some way. I designed an app where you can take photos of insects and the app tells you if the insect is good or bad for the plant. If it’s good the app seguests how you can attract more insects. If the insect is bad it tells you how to get rid of it in an environmentally friendly way.
